About GOATFarmer

GOATFarmer helps people who keep goats make better day-to-day decisions.

It works like advice from an experienced farmer. You can use it when goats are sick, not eating, giving less milk, or when you want to make cheese.

Why this helps

  • Works on simple phones
  • Does not need internet all the time
  • Gives clear steps, not long explanations
  • Helps you act early before problems get worse

How to use it

  • Tap a question below
  • Read the causes
  • Follow the steps

The advice is based on real goat farming knowledge about feeding, clean water, shelter, health, milk and cheese making.
