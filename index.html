About GOATFarmer

GOATFarmer is an offline-first agricultural assistant designed for small-scale and subsistence goat farmers.

It captures experienced goat-farming knowledge in a structured Knowledge Graph and delivers practical advice through a low-end, WhatsApp-style interface.

Why this matters

  • Many farmers work with limited or no internet access
  • Critical farming knowledge is often informal and unstructured
  • Early decisions prevent animal loss and low productivity

How it works

  • Farming knowledge is stored as structured JSON
  • Questions map to symptoms or actions
  • Advice is assembled through graph reasoning
  • No AI model or cloud service is required

This prototype demonstrates how applied AI techniques can be used to support real-world decision-making in low-resource environments.
