About GOATFarmer

GOATFarmer is a simple helper for people who keep goats. It gives advice the same way an experienced farmer would.

You can use it when your goat is not eating, giving less milk, or when you are not sure what to do.

Why GOATFarmer helps

  • Works on basic phones
  • Does not need internet all the time
  • Gives clear, practical advice
  • Helps you act early before problems get worse

How to use it

  • Tap a question at the bottom of the screen
  • Read the possible causes
  • Follow the steps shown

The advice comes from real goat farming knowledge about feeding, clean water, shelter, health and milk production.
